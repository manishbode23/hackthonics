Health Safety Tips

Follow these tips to stay healthy

Regular Exercise

Do at least 30 minutes of exercise daily to keep your heart healthy.

Healthy Diet

Eat fruits, vegetables, and avoid too much junk food.

Regular Health Checkup

Visit a doctor regularly to detect diseases early.

Avoid Smoking

Smoking increases the risk of cancer and heart disease.

Manage Stress

Practice meditation or yoga to reduce stress levels.

Stay Hydrated

Drink enough water daily to keep your body functioning properly.
